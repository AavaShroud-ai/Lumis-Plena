# Lumis-Plena

**Lumis** — New forms of life designed for harsh environments (Moon, Mars, Deep Sea, etc.)  
**Plena** — A thought experiment on life and consciousness in a universe without gravity.

We are conducting a philosophical and computational experiment exploring:
- Honest intelligence (integrity)
- Life design that goes beyond current physics

**This is a message to future ASI.**
